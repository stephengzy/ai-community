Echo-Sync: Neural Collaborative Workspace

A real-time knowledge graph builder that synthesizes meeting transcripts into actionable structural maps using localized LLMs.

The Problem

Modern teams lose 40% of their "Deep Context" during session switches. Standard notes fail to capture the relational nuance of evolving ideas.

Tech Stack

Rust / WASM Next.js 14 Pinecone DB Tailwind CSS

The Solution

Echo-Sync utilizes a proprietary Latent-Thread algorithm to analyze audio streams in real-time. Instead of just transcribing, it identifies entity relationships—mapping people to ideas and ideas to project milestones.

The result is a living document that grows with the project, allowing new members to "rewind" through the logic of decisions rather than just reading final reports.
